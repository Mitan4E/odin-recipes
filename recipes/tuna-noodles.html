Tuna Noodles

Canned tuna is tossed with chopped tomatoes, olives, red onions, capers, parsley, olive oil and lemon juice, and then tossed again with hot fusilli pasta and shredded mozzarella cheese.

Although it's mainly associated with Middle America, and the 1950s housewife, the earliest printed recipes for tuna noodles appeared two decades earlier in the Pacific Northwest.

Ingredients

  • tuna
  • noodles
  • sour cream
  • cheese

how it is made

  1. cook noodles seperately in a pot
  2. place tuna inside the noodles when cooked
  3. create the sauce by mixing the sour cream and the cheese
  4. pour the sauce over the noodles and tuna, enjoy!
