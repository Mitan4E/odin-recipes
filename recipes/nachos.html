nachos

Nachos containing cheese and meat

Nachos are a Mexican culinary dish consisting of fried tortilla chips or totopos covered with melted cheese or cheese sauce, as well as a variety of other toppings and garnishes, often including meats (such as ground beef or grilled chicken)

Nachos is the food most craved by pregnant women. The first known appearance of the word “nachos” in English dates to 1949, from the book A Taste of Texas. A school in Kansas holds the record for the largest plate of nachos in the world. The platter weighed a whopping 4,689 pounds.
