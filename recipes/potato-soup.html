Potato Soup

Potato soup is a hearty and comforting dish made with potatoes, broth, and milk or cream. It is typically seasoned with salt, pepper, and various herbs and spices, and can be garnished with toppings such as diced bacon or ham, shredded cheddar cheese, and chopped chives or green onions.

It is often served as a main dish, and can be enjoyed alone or with a side of bread or crackers. It is a versatile recipe that can be easily adapted to suit different tastes and preferences, making it a popular choice for many households. It is a easy to make and a budget friendly recipe that can be enjoyed any time of the year.

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Cream
  • Spring Onions
  • Bacon

How to make dish

  1. Mash potatos in a pot
  2. Add cream to the potato mash
  3. Add bacon into the mix
  4. Put dish in oven and top with spring onion when done
